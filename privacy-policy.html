Luca Benazzi has been working since 2003 as a UX consultant for small startups as well as corporations such as eBay, PayPal, Expedia, Thomson Reuters, Sky, Philips, to name a few. He's been testing out and reviewing every single piece of prototyping software available on the market (more than 100 products at the time of writing). Back in 2009, he created the most comprehensive showcase of Axure functionality available on the Internet, Axure in action. He's currently working on a brand new UX training program which is also going to include advanced Axure tutorials.
